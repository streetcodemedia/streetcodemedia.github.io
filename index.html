Own Your True Self, Your Career and Your Business

I understand the relationship between you and your business. Through integrative coaching, I guide you to utilize physical, mental and emotional energy, and recommend creative solutions to achieve peak performance and business effectiveness.

My services are right for you if you own or manage a business in any capacity, solopreneur to corporate, or are looking for guidance on how to begin your journey. I will help build customized roadmaps for success, break down barriers and counterproductive thinking, identify causes, and use individual/team strengths to overcome obstacles and deliver results. My enthusiastic personality and business insight has helped clients increase business effectiveness for over 25 years.

Through Implementing Change, I specialize in professional and entrepreneur coaching, management training, and organizational/personal development. As CEO of the Eastern Sierra Development and Training Institute (ESDTI), I am able to carry out my passion for working with businesses in gateway, resort and mountain towns.
